About Symbiotic Agro Sciences

Symbiotic Agro Sciences is a research-driven company dedicated to developing innovative biological solutions for agriculture. Our mission is to provide farmers with effective, sustainable tools that enhance crop productivity while protecting the environment.

With a team of experienced scientists and agronomists, we combine cutting-edge research with practical field experience to create products that deliver real results. Our solutions are designed to work in harmony with nature, promoting soil health and plant resilience.

Founded in 2015, Symbiotic Agro Sciences has quickly established itself as a leader in the agricultural biologicals sector, with products now used across North America and internationally.

Our Technology

At Symbiotic Agro Sciences, we combine advanced microbiology with innovative formulation technology to create products that are both highly effective and easy to use. Our proprietary strain development and fermentation processes ensure consistent, high-quality biological products.

We invest heavily in research and development, with state-of-the-art laboratories and field testing programs that validate product performance under real-world conditions. Our scientific team is constantly exploring new microbial strains and biological solutions to address emerging agricultural challenges.
