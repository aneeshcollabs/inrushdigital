Our Studio

Welcome to Serenity Yoga, a sanctuary in the heart of the city where you can escape the hustle and reconnect with yourself. Our studio was founded in 2010 with the vision of creating a welcoming space for yogis of all levels.

We believe yoga is for everybody and every body. Our experienced teachers guide you through practices that strengthen not just your body, but also your mind and spirit.

Our light-filled studio features eco-friendly bamboo floors, top-quality props, and a peaceful atmosphere that will help you leave the outside world behind.
